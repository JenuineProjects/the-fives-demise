THE FIVE'S DEMISE

Block World Edition

HOW TO PLAY

GOAL: The evil number 5 is hunting you! Collect numbers 1, 2, 3, and 4 hidden across four zones to forge an axe and destroy it.

FIND THE NUMBERS:

1 - on a lily pad in the Water Zone (blue blocks)

2 - on a camel in the Desert Zone (yellow blocks)

3 - on a snake in the Forest Zone (green blocks)

4 - on a rock in the Rocky Zone (grey blocks)

RULES:

- You have 3 hearts. If the 5 catches you, you lose a heart and drop all collected numbers!

- Lose all 3 hearts and it's game over.

- Collect all 4 numbers to forge an AXE.

- Get close to the 5 and press F to strike and win!

- Watch out: the 5 gets faster over time!

- Be careful near edges - you can fall off the map!

CONTROLS:

W A S D - Move (forward / left / back / right)

Mouse - Look around

Space - Jump

F - Attack with axe (when forged)
